In June 2010, Harrelson took part in Soccer Aid at Old Trafford in Manchester to raise money for UNICEF.

Harrelson is also an environmental activist. He has attended environmental events such as the PICNIC'07 festival that was held in Amsterdam, The Netherlands, for three days in September 2007.

Harrelson identifies as an anarchist

Woody Harrelson Wiki Page
  • Harrelson's big break came in 1985, when he was cast as sweet, dim-witted bartender Woody Boyd on the wildly popular sitcom Cheers
  • Harrelson earned the attention of critics again in 2007 for the Coen brothers drama No Country for Old Men.
  • His performance earned him five Emmy nominations, including a 1989 Emmy win for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Comedy Series.
  • One of his more prominent roles on the small screen was made in January 2014, with the HBO series True Detective.
  • In addition to acting, Harrelson has been an outspoken advocate for the environment.
